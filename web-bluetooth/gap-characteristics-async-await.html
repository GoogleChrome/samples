This sample illustrates the use of the Web Bluetooth API to read all GAP characteristics from a nearby Bluetooth Device. You may want to try this demo with the BLE Peripheral Simulator App from the Google Play Store and check out the GAP Characteristics (Promises) sample.

Note: The Reconnection Address characteristic is blocklisted and forbidden from being accessed.
