Background

PaymentRequest lets you accept payment from different payment methods.

  • Credit cards - a sample that accepts credit card payments and does not request shipping information.
  • Android Pay - a sample that accepts Android Pay payments and does not request shipping information.
  • Free shipping - a sample that accepts credit card payments and provides free shipping worldwide.
  • Shipping options - a sample that accepts credit card payments and provides a couple of shipping options regardless of shipping address.
  • Dynamic shipping - a sample that accepts credit card payments and varies the availability and price of shipping options depending on the shipping address.
  • Contact info - a sample that accepts credit card payments and requests user's contact information: name, phone number, and email address.
  • Can make payment - a sample that accepts both Android Pay and credit card payments and checks whether the browser can make payment.
  • Server side - sample that demonstrates server-side calculation of shipping price and processing transactions through a payment service provider.
  • Stickersheet - A stickersheet that allows you to design a payments flow in Sketch App.
