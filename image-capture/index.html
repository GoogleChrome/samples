The following samples show you some of the ways that you can use the Image Capture API.

Note: Starting in Chrome 56, it is also available for trials so that websites can use it without any flag.

Samples

Grab Frame - Take Photo - capture images in the form of a Blob or as a ImageBitmap.

Update Camera Zoom - change the zoom setting of the camera.
